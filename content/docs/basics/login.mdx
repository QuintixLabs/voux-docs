## Logging in

Your **Voux** installation will create a default administrator account with the username `admin` and the password `change-this-password`.

The first thing you will want to do is **log in** and **change your password**.

<img
  src="/assets/docs/login.png"

  style={{ maxWidth: '420px', width: '100%' }}
/>

---

## Opening your profile

After logging in, open the profile menu in the top right and click on **Profile**.

<img
  src="/assets/docs/profile.png"
  alt="Voux login screen"
  style={{ maxWidth: '220px', width: '100%' }}
/>

---

## Changing your password and username

Finally, click **Reset Password** to change your password, then make sure to update your **username** as well.
